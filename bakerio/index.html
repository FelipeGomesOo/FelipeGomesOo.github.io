Our Mission

To delight our customers with the authentic flavors and warmth of Brazilian baked goods.

We are dedicated to using the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create delicious and traditional pastries, bread, and cakes that satisfy the taste buds and nourish the soul.

Our goal is to provide a welcoming and comfortable environment for our customers, where they can experience the rich cultural heritage of Brazil through our baked goods

Our team

Our team is made up of skilled bakers and pastry chefs who are passionate about creating delicious and authentic Brazilian baked goods.

With years of experience in baking, João leads our team in creating the most delicious and softest bread in town.

Maria is responsible for our delicious cakes and sweets. She is creative and always seeks to innovate, without losing the essence of Brazilian tradition.

Carlos André's smile greets our customers every day, with friendliness and cordiality. He knows all our products and is always ready to help.
